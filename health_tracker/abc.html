About BodySync

Your personal digital health companion.

What is BodySync?

BodySync is a user-friendly health tracking platform where users can monitor their BMI, water intake, sleep duration, and calorie consumption with simple tools and a clean interface.

Our Mission

To help users build a healthier lifestyle through simple, effective and accessible digital tools. BodySync motivates users to track their daily habits and make informed health decisions.

Features

  • BMI Calculation
  • Daily Water Intake Tracking
  • Sleep Duration Monitoring
  • Calorie Count Tracking
  • Personal Dashboard Summary
